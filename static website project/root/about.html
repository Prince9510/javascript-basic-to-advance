Who We Are

Dr London Med Strategix is a leading healthcare recruitment and career development consultancy, specializing in helping healthcare professionals navigate their career paths both in the UK and internationally.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between talented healthcare professionals and their dream careers, we provide comprehensive support services that go beyond traditional recruitment.

  • Personalized career guidance and planning
  • Professional CV and cover letter creation
  • Interview preparation and mock sessions
  • Visa and immigration support
  • Relocation assistance and cultural integration
  • Ongoing career development and training
